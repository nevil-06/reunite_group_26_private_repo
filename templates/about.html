About Us

Welcome to ReUnite

ReUnite is an online marketplace that connects students on campus to promote the reuse of goods, fostering a sustainable and community-driven environment. Our platform aims to reduce waste, save resources, and encourage a culture of responsibility and mutual assistance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support sustainability and strengthen community ties by creating a space for the exchange of items among students and faculty. We believe that small, community-driven actions can lead to significant positive change.

Our Team

Our team is dedicated to leveraging technology for environmental and social benefits. We are passionate about sustainability and committed to making a positive impact on our campus and beyond.
